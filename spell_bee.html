Spell Bee

  • Details

Spell Bee is a famous word-spelling game in which players attempt to spell correctly and aloud words assigned to them. Words are given with simple definitions with references and dictionaries to help you quickly find more expanded and accurate definitions. It's purpose is to help students improve their spelling, increase their vocabularies, learn concepts and develop correct English usage that will help them all their lives.

Rules-

  • 1. One person can participate only once. i.e. Re-Registration is not allowed.
  • 2. The participant will be given five words to spell, each with a different level of difficulty.
  • 3. Words will be taken from Engineering fields like Software, Electronic etc.
  • 4. Participant can choose the field in the beginning and words related to it will be given to him.
  • 5. No change of field is allowed in the middle of game.
  • 6. The participant may ask the pronouncer to say the word again or ask for a basic definition.
  • 7. The participant will be given twenty seconds to spell each word.
  • 8. The 20 seconds begin when the pronouncer/organiser pronounces the word.
  • 9. Points will be awarded on the basis of the number of correctly spelled words. Eg: 2 correct words- 2 Points 5 correct words- 5 Points
  • 10. Prizes will be given based on the number of points procured by the participant.
  • 11. Use of electronic device is strictly prohibited and will result in direct disqualification.
  • 12. The decision of the pronouncer/organiser will be final and binding.
