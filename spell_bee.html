Spell Bee

  • Details
  • Venue

Spell Bee is a famous word-spelling game in which players attempt to spell correctly and aloud words assigned to them. Words are given with simple definitions with references and dictionaries to help you quickly find more expanded and accurate definitions. It's purpose is to help students improve their spelling, increase their vocabularies, learn concepts and develop correct English usage that will help them all their lives.

Rules-

  • 1. A team of 2 players will be allowed.
  • 2. There will be 3 rounds.
  • 3. Words will be repeated at max 5 times.
  • 4. Only 60 sec will be given for each word.
  • 5. college students of all years are allowed.
  • 6. General definition and reference of words will be given on demand.
  • 7. Use of pen, paper, electronic gadgets etc is strictly prohibited and will result in direct disqualification.
  • 8. Each team will be given 5 words in each round.
