Placing bets, solving puzzles, earning money. Sounds fun? ASN presents to you, CODE GAMBLE, a betting based event, where your puzzle solving and gambling skills will earn you points and cash

Rules-

  • 1. This is an onsite event. Participate as a lone wolf or in a team of 2.
  • 2. All the teams will be given a certain number of poker chips at the beginning in exchange for the entry fee.
  • 3. The event will consist of various rounds.
  • a) At the start of a round, a question will be displayed on the project screen and you can attempt the question by betting.
  • b) Depending on whether your team can solve the question or not, you can choose to ""Raise"" the bet, ""Call"" a previous bet or even ""Fold"" the round.
  • c) The team that solves the question fastest wins the round and takes the ""Pot"".
  • d) If no team is able to solve the question within the given time, then the points are given to the house (organisers).
  • 4. You can exchange your poker chips for cash anytime you want."
