Nox Caelo - A Star-Gazing Experience

Our Earth, our Solar System, even our galaxy is nothing more than a small part of the Cosmos in which we reside. As inquisitive and curious man may be, we still haven't decoded the Cosmos for what it is What lies out there? Out of the fraction of the Universe that we can observe, how much can we actually see? We here at Nakshatra strive to answer such questions that perplex most people. After all, the best way to understand something is to first look at it and admire it's beauty and acknowledge it's place in the Universe. To explore the night sky, we humbly invite you witness the pompuos show put on by none other than the Cosmos Are you ready to get amazed by the beauty and vastness of the Universe ?

Rules-

  • 1. Use of mobile phones is prohibited on the premises, anyone seen with an open flashlight or bright screen shall be immediately removed from the venue
  • 2. If absolutely necessary, use the mobile phone with screens on minimum brightness separate from the group.
  • 3. Cameras are allowed on the premises, but make sure to turn the flashlight off.
  • 4. Follow the instructions given by the organizers, they will guide you from one part of the event to another.
  • 5. All participants are requested to respect the queue and maintain decorum.
  • 6. No misbehavior will be tolerated, violators will immediately be escorted out of the premises.
  • 7. No participant is allowed to handle the telescopes without the permission of the organizers.
